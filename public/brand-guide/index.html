BitPopArt — Brand Guide

Bitcoin Pop Art by Johannes Oppewal · bitpopart.com · @bitpopart

This guide documents the complete visual identity, color system, typography, and UI design language of BitPopArt — the Bitcoin Pop Art platform built on Nostr.

BitPopArt
Bitcoin Pop Art · Nostr · Lightning
Every block. A unique piece of art. ₿

Brand Personality

Bold. Colorful. Pop. Bitcoin-native. Art is generated deterministically from block data — the same block always gets the same art. Every 10 minutes, a new piece. Shared on Nostr. Zapped with Lightning.
