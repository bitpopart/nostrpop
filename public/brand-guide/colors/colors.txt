  from #f97316 to #ea580c  (orange-500 → orange-600)
  CSS: bg-gradient-to-r from-orange-500 to-orange-600

Nostr Publish / Share (orange → pink):
  from #f97316 to #ec4899  (orange-500 → pink-500)
  CSS: bg-gradient-to-r from-orange-500 to-pink-500
  Hover: from-orange-600 to-pink-600

Hero Text Gradient (orange → yellow → orange):
  from #f97316 via #facc15 to #f97316  (orange → yellow → orange)
  CSS: bg-gradient-to-r from-orange-500 via-yellow-400 to-orange-500
  Apply: bg-clip-text text-transparent

Page Background (light):
  from orange-950/10 via background to orange-950/5
  CSS: bg-gradient-to-b from-orange-950/10 via-background to-orange-950/5

Block Art Canvas (dark):
  from #431407 to #7c2d12  (orange-950 → orange-900)
  CSS: bg-gradient-to-br from-orange-950 to-orange-900

Fear & Greed accent divider:
  from transparent via orange-300 to transparent
  CSS: bg-gradient-to-r from-transparent via-orange-300 to-transparent
